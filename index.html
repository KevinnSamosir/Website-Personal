About Me

I am an Information Systems student with strong interests and competencies in software engineering, system analysis, data analysis, and web development. I am accustomed to developing data-driven and user-oriented digital solutions using a systematic and analytical approach to translate business requirements into effective and scalable system and software designs.

In my role as a System Analyst and Data Analyst, I have experience in requirements analysis, business process modeling, and data processing to ensure that the developed systems operate efficiently, accurately, and in alignment with organizational objectives. My technical expertise includes System Analysis & Design (BPMN, ERD, and DFD), Data Analysis & Management (SQL, MySQL, and data modeling), as well as web development using Laravel with an MVC architecture, supported by Front-End and Back-End skills and a solid understanding of UI/UX design principles.

Through academic experience and involvement in various projects, I am trained in producing clear system documentation, generating data-driven analysis and insights, and designing structured and practical technical solutions. I highly value teamwork, analytical thinking, and the application of best practices in system development and implementation.

Currently, I am actively involved in various Information Systems projects, including the development of a digital waste bank system, focusing on requirements analysis, system design, data-driven decision-making, and effective cross-functional collaboration.
